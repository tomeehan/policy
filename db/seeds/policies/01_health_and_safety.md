**POLICY STATEMENT**

This policy outlines the commitment of Acme Care Services to ensuring the health, safety and welfare of all employees, service users, and visitors. We recognise our duty under the Health and Safety at Work Act and related legislation to provide a safe working environment.

**AIM OF THE POLICY**

This policy is intended to set out the values, principles and procedures underpinning Acme Care Services' approach to health and safety management. We believe that effective health and safety management is essential to the delivery of high-quality care services.

### Responsibilities

**Directors and Senior Managers**

a) Ensure that adequate resources are allocated to meet health and safety requirements

b) Review health and safety performance regularly and take corrective action where necessary

c) Lead by example in following safe working practices

**All Staff**

a) Take reasonable care of their own health and safety and that of others who may be affected by their actions

b) Cooperate with the organisation in matters of health and safety

c) Report any hazards, accidents or near-misses immediately

d) Use equipment and materials in accordance with training and instructions provided

### Risk Assessment

Acme Care Services will conduct regular risk assessments of all work activities. These assessments will:

- Identify potential hazards
- Evaluate the risks arising from those hazards
- Implement appropriate control measures
- Be reviewed and updated regularly

### Training

All staff will receive appropriate health and safety training, including:

- Induction training covering basic health and safety principles
- Manual handling training
- Fire safety awareness
- First aid training for designated first aiders
- Specific training related to their role

### Monitoring and Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or sooner if there are significant changes to legislation, guidance, or our operations.
