**POLICY STATEMENT**

Acme Care Services is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of all adults and children who use our services or come into contact with our organisation. We recognise that everyone has a right to live free from abuse, neglect and exploitation.

**AIM OF THE POLICY**

This policy sets out our commitment to safeguarding and provides guidance on recognising, responding to, and reporting safeguarding concerns. All staff have a responsibility to be aware of the signs of abuse and to report concerns promptly.

### Types of Abuse

Staff should be aware of the following types of abuse:

- **Physical abuse** - hitting, slapping, pushing, restraint, misuse of medication
- **Emotional/psychological abuse** - threats, humiliation, controlling behaviour, isolation
- **Financial abuse** - theft, fraud, exploitation, pressure regarding wills or property
- **Sexual abuse** - any sexual act without consent
- **Neglect** - failure to meet basic needs, withholding necessities
- **Discriminatory abuse** - harassment based on protected characteristics
- **Organisational abuse** - poor care standards, rigid routines, inadequate staffing
- **Self-neglect** - inability or unwillingness to care for oneself

### Recognising Abuse

Signs of abuse may include:

- Unexplained injuries or bruising
- Changes in behaviour or mood
- Withdrawal or fearfulness
- Poor hygiene or unexplained weight loss
- Missing money or possessions
- Reluctance to be alone with certain individuals

### Reporting Concerns

If you suspect abuse or a service user discloses abuse:

a) Listen carefully and take the concern seriously

b) Do not promise confidentiality - explain you may need to share information

c) Record what you have seen or been told accurately and promptly

d) Report immediately to the Designated Safeguarding Lead

e) Do not investigate yourself

### Multi-Agency Working

We will work collaboratively with local authority safeguarding teams, the police, and other agencies to protect vulnerable individuals and share information appropriately.

### Training

All staff will receive safeguarding training at induction and regular refresher training thereafter.
