**POLICY STATEMENT**

Acme Care Services is committed to promoting equality of opportunity and eliminating discrimination in all aspects of our work. We value diversity and believe that a diverse workforce enriches our organisation and improves the quality of care we provide.

**LEGAL FRAMEWORK**

This policy is underpinned by the Equality Act 2010, which protects individuals from discrimination on the grounds of:

- Age
- Disability
- Gender reassignment
- Marriage and civil partnership
- Pregnancy and maternity
- Race
- Religion or belief
- Sex
- Sexual orientation

### Our Commitments

**In Employment**

We will:

a) Recruit and select staff based on merit and ability

b) Provide equal access to training, development and promotion opportunities

c) Ensure pay and conditions are applied fairly

d) Not tolerate harassment, bullying or victimisation

e) Make reasonable adjustments for disabled employees

**In Service Delivery**

We will:

a) Treat all service users with dignity and respect

b) Provide services that are accessible and responsive to individual needs

c) Ensure care plans reflect personal, cultural and religious preferences

d) Challenge discriminatory behaviour or attitudes

e) Work to eliminate barriers to accessing our services

### Responsibilities

**All Staff**

- Treat colleagues and service users with respect
- Challenge discrimination when they see it
- Attend equality and diversity training
- Report any concerns about discrimination

**Managers**

- Promote an inclusive culture
- Address discrimination promptly and effectively
- Monitor equality data and take action to address disparities
- Ensure recruitment and selection processes are fair

### Monitoring and Review

We will monitor our workforce profile and service user demographics to identify and address any inequalities. This policy will be reviewed annually.
